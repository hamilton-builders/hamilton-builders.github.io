Our Legacy

I was fortunate to learn this business from childhood by working for my father, Frank Hamilton, a highly respected homebuilder of Longview. Since forming my own company in 1990, I have been further blessed by success in work that I love and that enhances my home town. I hold the Graduate Master Builder designation from the National Association of Homebuilders, and my employees and associates are similarly qualified in their respective fields. To stay abreast of current methods, materials, and styles, we regularly attend professional seminars and trade shows. Our headquarters consists of offices, shops, and warehouses on the same tract as my own home at 1625 East Loop 281. We have built hundreds of dwellings in and near Longview, many of them in subdivisions that we developed ourselves—most recently Pecan Crest (off of Airline Drive north of Lobo Stadium) and the Great Woods (north of the loop off of Tryon).

Though we accept projects of any scope within a full spectrum of services, our primary focus is on turnkey custom design and construction of unique homes for clients who appreciate high standards of taste and quality. That emphasis earned us a place among 100 similar firms in the Custom Builder Program of Southern Living Magazine. We like to think that the enviable longevity and growth of the company have been due not only to the satisfaction of our clients, but also to careful and conservative management of our human and financial resources. Scott Hamilton Custom Homes is a member of Guild Quality. Like other top builders who are members, we are committed to making sure our customers get the best experience possible while working with us. Guild Quality is a third-party survey service that enhances our ability to maintain the highest standards of customer service.

As recipients of the "Locals Love Us" award all 5 years the award has been given and winners of "Best of East Texas" 5 of 6 years, the Hamilton team is dedicated to maintaining excellence and providing an experience our clients will enjoy from start to finish.

We are also an accredited member of the Better Business Bureau of East Texas. This means that we have made a commitment to do our job in a trustworthy, honorable, and transparent manner. The accreditation also means that we will resolve any complaints expeditiously. We have been a recipient of a BBB Gold Star award for the past 20 years for being complaint free.
